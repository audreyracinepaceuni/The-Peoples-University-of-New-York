Welcome to the People's University of New York!

The People's University of New York is a globally recognized institution, dedicated to new and emerging technologies. Our university offers limited, but strong and intensive course paths, specializing in Technical and Education degrees at the undergraduate and graduate level.

About Us

At PUNY, we praise dedication, innovation, and collaboration, and recognize to be the best, we must spend our resources on strong programs. The structure of our institution is separated in two parts, the Tate College of Technology, and the Lewis College of Education.

The Tate College of Technology

Our institution is immensely proud of our staff and instructors, as many are doctors, innovators, and have years of work experience in the technical world.

We offer events such as Hackathon, Robotics Competitions, app development competitions, and more. We celebrate collaboration and competition, as we believe it cultivates a creative environment.

The Lewis College of Education

Our college is dedicated preserving our history and strengthening our future teachers. We know that there is no better investment in our future than in the education for our youth.

Our school focuses on hands-on experiences and training, and prides itself in its role in helping the local communities. Students participate in fieldwork that is meaningful and local, and can start as early as their second semester!

To learn more about our schools, visit our Departments page.

Tours

Tours can be scheduled through the Contact Us page, and are available year-round. Our admissions office offers group tours and seminars, and visitors are welcome to explore on their own.
