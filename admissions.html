Admissions Overview

At The People’s University of New York, we seek students who demonstrate academic excellence, creativity, and a commitment to community impact. Our streamlined admissions process makes it easy to apply to both the Tate College of Technology and the Lewis College of Education.

Application Requirements

  • Completed Application Form
  • Official Transcripts from all prior institutions
  • Two Letters of Recommendation
  • Personal Statement (500–750 words)
  • Resume or CV
  • Application Fee (waivers available)
